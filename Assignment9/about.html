Our Story

Nestled in the heart of Seattle, our bakery is dedicated to bringing you the most delicious chocolate chip cookies you've ever tasted. Our family-owned bakery started as a small hobby, crafting the perfect chocolate chip cookie, and has since grown into a local favorite.

At Seattle's Sweetest Bakery, we believe in using only the finest ingredients freshly made butter, rich dark chocolate, and locally sourced flour. We bake each batch with love and attention to detail, ensuring that every cookie is soft, chewy, and full of flavor. Whether you're stopping by for a quick treat or ordering a dozen for a special event, we promise you’ll always leave with a smile.

We are passionate about our cookies, and we take great pride in being part of the vibrant Seattle community. Join us for a cup of coffee and the best chocolate chip cookie in town. We can’t wait to share our sweet creations with you!

Visit us today and experience the warmth and sweetness of Seattle’s Sweetest Bakery. We’re located at 123 Cookie Lane, Seattle, WA.
